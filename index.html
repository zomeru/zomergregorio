    01.About me

    Hello! My name is Zomer Gregorio, a college student from STI College Alabang and I enjoy building things that live on the internet. My interest in web development started way back in 2017 when I downloaded a HTML and CSS Portfolio template and hosted it with free domain. I had very little knowledge about HTML and CSS that time that even changing the theme color was very difficult for me and so slow to catch up with online tutorials. After that, my frustration made me lose my interest in web development.

    But, in 2019, after I enrolled in college. I got my interest back in web development and that was when my journey in web development begun. I started learning through online courses and Youtube tutorials. Fast forward today, I now have strong knowledge in front-end development and started learning back-end development. I really love programming and solving problems because I think it makes me a better person, I even quit as a casual gamer to focus on my programming career. Beside programming, I am also interested in sports like running and basketball.
